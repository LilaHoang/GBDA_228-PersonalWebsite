About me

Hello, my name is Lila Hoang, I am passionate designer with a focus
on graphic design and UX design. I am fascinated by the intersection of
visual communication and user experience, striving to create designs that
not only captivate but also provide seamless and intuitive interactions.
Currently pursuing my studies at the University of Waterloo in the Global
Business and Digital Arts (GBDA) program, I am on a journey to merge my
love for design with a deep understanding of global business dynamics.

I am excited about the possibility of contributing my skills and creativity to
exciting projects. Whether you're looking for compelling graphics or a
seamless user experience, let's connect and explore the possibilities
together.
